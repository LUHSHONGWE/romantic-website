For Nontobeko (Thatho) 🌹

From the moment I got to know you, something about you felt special. Your smile, your kindness, and the way you carry yourself — it all stands out to me.

I really like you. And if life ever gave me the chance, I would want to learn how to love you the right way — the way God intended for you to be loved: with respect, patience, and honesty.

Right now I’m focusing on my studies and building my future. But maybe one day, when the time is right, I hope you might think about the possibility of us.

Bengingathanda ukuthi ngelinye ilanga ucabange ngathi.

Because honestly… I think we could make an amazing couple. 🌹
